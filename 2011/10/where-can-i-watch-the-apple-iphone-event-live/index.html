Where Can I Watch the Apple iPhone Event Live?

by Ray Hollister • 10/04/2011 • 5 Comments

Wondering where you can watch a live video stream of today’s Apple iPhone event. In a word, the answer is: nowhere. Even though “Let’s Talk iPhone” is being held at Apple’s headquarters (where it is safe to assume there are a plethora or computers handy), and even though there are more people interested in this event than any previous Apple keynote events, it  is not being streamed on the internet. As far as we know, no journalists will be broadcasting videos from inside the event either. So, this made me wonder, why? Did Justin.tv not have the bandwidth to handle it? Did Ustream.tv refuse to give Apple the entire frontpage of the website? Was CBS too busy at the Adobe event? Got any theories? Share them in the comments below. Also, what sites are going to be live blogging the event today? Where will you be catching your iMorsels as they drop from the keyboards of the journalists that are attending the event?
